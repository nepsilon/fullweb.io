F.A.Q.

What is Fullweb?

Fullweb is a weekly newsletter. It gives you the best 5 links every week about Backend, Web Frontend, DevOps and User Experience.

Who need to read it?

Fullweb primarily targets Full-Stack Developers, and anybody who need to stay relevant on a wide scope of technologies.

Fullweb is also read by non-tech people, working in tech environment, who want to acquire a good general knowledge on what the tech world is about today.

Where are the links curated from?

The links are curated from social sites like HN, DN, Reddit, Quora, tech blogs like TechCrunch, PandoDaily, and also other newsletters and podcasts.

When do I receive the links?

Every Tuesday 8am.

Is it free?

Yes.
