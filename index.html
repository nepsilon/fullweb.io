F.A.Q.

What is Fullweb?

Fullweb is a weekly newsletter. It gives you the best 5 links every week on Backend, Web Frontend, Data Science, User experience and Entrepreneurship. One link for each category each week. Nothing else.

Who need to read it?

Fullweb primarily targets Full-Stack Developers, Directors of Engineering, and anybody who need to stay relevant on a wide scope of technologies.

Fullweb is also read by non-tech people, working in tech environment, who want to acquire a good general knowledge on what the tech world is about today.

Where are the links curated from?

The links are curated from any tech media whose publish in English. Including social sites like HN, DN, Reddit, tech blogs like techcrunch, pandodaily, but also other newsletters and podcasts.

When do I receive the links?

Every Tuesday 9am.

Is it free?

Yes.

Can I unsubscribe at any time?

Yes. Every email we send contains a link to unsubscribe.
