PROTIP OF THE WEEK
 

How to convert PSD to PNG with Node.js

A quick tip from the David Walsh’s blog, explaining us how to use psd.js to convert PSDs to PNG, but also extract lots of metadata. It looks like this: 

var PSD = require('psd');
 
PSD.open('homepage.psd').then(function (psd) {
  return psd.image.saveAsPng('homepage.png');
}).then(function () {
  console.log('Finished!');
});
