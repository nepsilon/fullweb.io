TIP OF THE WEEK

A short script this week to replace spaces in filenames in a given folder:

# Looping over files, # forging new filename # and then renaming it: for oldname in *
do
  newname=`echo $oldname | sed -e 's/ /_/g'`
  mv "$oldname" "$newname"
done # Spaces are now underscores
# Remove spaces with this # sed expression: ... | sed -e 's/ //g'

# Append this tr expression to sed # to lowercase filenames: ... | tr '[:upper:]' '[:lower:]'
