How to kill a frozen SSH session?

No need to close your terminal tab anymore (or furiously hitting your keyboard) when your SSH session freeze. SSH has an escape sequence that will let you properly close the connection and give you back the prompt. In order, type: 

[Return Key]
~
.

That‚Äôs it! This will send an escape sequence to your local SSH socket and close the connection.
